List of highest-grossing South Indian films

1.Baahubali 2: The Conclusion

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion is a 2017 Indian epic action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli, who co-wrote the script with V. Vijayendra Prasad. It was produced by Shobu Yarlagadda and Prasad Devineni under the banner Arka Media Works. Produced in the Telugu film industry,the film was shot in both Telugu and Tamil languages.The cast includes Prabhas in a dual role, alongside Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, Tamannaah Bhatia, Ramya Krishnan, Sathyaraj, Nassar and Subbaraju. It is both the sequel and the prequel to Baahubali: The Beginning, as well as the final installment in the Baahubali film duology. The film is set in fictional medieval India and centers on the rivalry between siblings Amarendra Baahubali and Bhallaladeva. Bhallaladeva conspires against Amarendra, leading to his death at the hands of Kattappa. Years later, Amarendra's son (Mahendra or Sivudu) seeks to avenge his father's demise."Baahubali 2: The Conclusion" was a massive box office success, grossing over ₹1,810.60 crore worldwide. It earned ₹1,429.83 crore in India and ₹380.77 crore overseas, becoming one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time. The Hindi version alone earned an impressive ₹490 crore.

2.Pushpa 2: The Rule

"Pushpa 2: The Rule" has achieved massive success at the box office, grossing over ₹1,800 crore worldwide. The Hindi version alone has surpassed ₹800 crore, setting a new benchmark for Indian cinema. The film is on track to become one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time, potentially challenging Baahubali 2's record. Pushpa 2: The Rule is a 2024 Indian Telugu-language action drama film[7] written and directed by Sukumar and produced by Mythri Movie Makers in association with Sukumar Writings. A sequel to Pushpa: The Rise (2021), it is the second installment in the Pushpa film series. The film stars Allu Arjun in the titular role, alongside Rashmika Mandanna, Fahadh Faasil, Jagapathi Babu, Sunil and Rao Ramesh. It follows Pushpa Raj, a labourer-turned-red sandalwood smuggler, as he faces growing threats from his enemies, including SP Bhanwar Singh Shekhawat.

3.RRR

The Indian movie "RRR" has achieved significant box office success, grossing ₹1,387 crore worldwide. It became the fourth highest-grossing Indian film, the third highest-grossing Telugu film, and the highest-grossing Indian film of 2022. The film crossed the ₹1,000 crore mark in 16 days and earned ₹1,100 crore in its first 28 days.

4.KGF: Chapter 2

KGF: Chapter 2 has grossed over ₹1,233 crore worldwide, making it the second highest-grossing Indian film of all time, according to Wikipedia and Box Office India. It is the highest-grossing Indian film of 2022, according to Wikipedia and Box Office India. The film's worldwide gross includes over ₹800 crore in India alone. The movie also earned over ₹375 crore in South India, excluding the Hindi version.

5.Kalki 2898 AD

The Indian mythological sci-fi film "Kalki 2898 AD" has grossed an estimated ₹1,042.25 crore worldwide, making it the seventh-highest-grossing Indian film globally, according to Sacnilk. In India, it earned ₹646.25 crore net and ₹767.25 crore gross, according to Sacnilk. The film also achieved a significant overseas collection of ₹275 crore, according to Sacnilk.

6.2.O

The 2018 film "2.O" starring Rajinikanth and Akshay Kumar had a strong box office performance, grossing over Rs 650 crore worldwide. The film reached a historic Rs 700 crore milestone and is considered a blockbuster. In India, it grossed over Rs 400 crore. The film was also a success in overseas markets, earning over Rs 172 crore.

7.Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire

The film "Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire" has grossed ₹720 crore worldwide. In India, the film earned ₹404.87 crore net and ₹486 crore gross, with the Hindi version contributing significantly to the overseas collection. The film, directed by Prashanth Neel and starring Prabhas, was released in theaters on December 22.

8.Baahubali: The Beginning

The total worldwide gross collection for "Baahubali: The Beginning" (Part 1) is approximately ₹650 crore (US$92 million). This includes collections from all languages and all markets. The film's Hindi dubbed version alone grossed over ₹1 billion (100 crore), making it the first South Indian and first non-Hindi film to achieve that milestone in Hindi.

9.Jailer

The movie "Jailer" starring Rajinikanth grossed ₹606.50 crores worldwide. This includes a gross of ₹407 crores in India and ₹197.9 crores overseas, according to IMDb. It was a major success for Rajinikanth, marking a comeback after a string of underperforming films.

10.Leo

The movie "Leo" starring Thalapathy Vijay grossed over ₹600 crore worldwide. It's one of the highest-grossing Tamil films of all time, and the highest-grossing Tamil film of 2023. In Tamil Nadu, it grossed over ₹230 crore. The film also performed well in overseas markets, grossing over ₹199 crore.

11.Devara: Part 1

The movie "Devara: Part 1" has grossed a total of ₹421.63 crore worldwide. This includes ₹292.03 crore in India (net collection). Sacnilk reports that the film's worldwide gross reached ₹500 crore within 21 days of its release.
